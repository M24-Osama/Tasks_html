Linear Equations

An equation that has a maximum degree of one is known as a linear equation. The graph of it is a straight line.

For example:

  • y = 8x - 2
  • ax1 + bx2 - cx3 = 9

These are some examples of linear equations in two and three variables. Slope of Intersection of Two Linear Equations

y1 = mx1 + a and
y2 = mx2 + b

For the points P(x1, y1) and Q(x2, y2) is:

m = (y2 - y1) / (x2 - x1)
